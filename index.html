About Me

Hello! I am Hafiz Mustafa Amin, a dedicated Game Developer and Metaverse Specialist based in Lahore. For me, game development isn't just about writing code. it's about engineering experiences that leave a lasting impact. My journey sits at the intersection of technical precision and creative storytelling, where I use tools like Unity, C#, and the Spatial.io SDK to bridge the gap between physical reality and digital imagination.


Currently, I am driving innovation as an Associate Game Developer at Finz Games, working on industry standard projects that push the limits of interactive entertainment. Prior to this, I made my mark by developing the award-winning "Virtual UMT" Metaverse project. This endeavor, built using Unity and Spatial, not only digitally transformed the university experience but also earned me the prestigious Rector Award, highlighting my ability to lead complex technical projects from concept to execution.

Beyond the screen, I am a leader. Serving as the Director of Events for the Mindstorm Student Chapter, I have cultivated a community of like-minded developers, fostering collaboration and growth.
